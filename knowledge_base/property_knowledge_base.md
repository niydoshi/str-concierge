## Local Recommendations
Here's a local guide to Smith Mountain Lake, Virginia, covering the best places to eat, explore, and enjoy the outdoors:

🍽️ Restaurants

Napoli By the Lake ⭐ 4.7 — The top fine dining pick on the lake. Known for jumbo crab cakes, Chilean sea bass, and exceptional service. Open Thu–Sun evenings; reservations are a must.

Tiff's Lakefront ⭐ 4.3 — An upscale-but-relaxed lakefront spot with gorgeous water views, a rotating menu, and a fun trivia night. Accessible by car or boat. Open Thu–Sat.

Drifter's ⭐ 4.4 — The go-to casual waterfront hangout with BBQ, seafood, live music, and dock parking for your boat. Open Fri–Sun.

The Copper Kettle Co. ⭐ 4.2 — A cozy dinner restaurant with a nice patio, known for steaks, seafood, and seared tuna. Open most evenings Wed–Sun.

🌿 Outdoors & Activities

Smith Mountain Lake State Park ⭐ 4.7 — The crown jewel of the area. Hiking trails, kayak and pontoon rentals, a swimming beach, RV camping, and stunning mountain-backed lake views.

Smith Mountain Lake Community Park ⭐ 4.7 — A hidden gem with a lake beach, fishing pier, walking trails, kayak launch, and playground. Largely free and great for families.

Smith Mountain Lake Beach ⭐ 4.5 — A relaxed sandy beach (inside the state park) for swimming and picnicking. Just $4–5 per person, open daily 11am–6pm.

Spring Valley Farm ⭐ 4.9 — Highly rated horseback trail rides through pastures and forested land. Also offers tiny home stays. Book ahead — this one fills up fast!
